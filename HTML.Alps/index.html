Crossing The Alps

What would it feel like to cross the Alps on foot in just one week? A few years ago, I experienced exactly that journey from Oberstdorf to Meran.

The Experience

Crossing the Alps was a six-day journey filled with constant change and unforgettable scenery. From dramatic high mountains and snow-covered glaciers to peaceful valleys in Austria, each day offered something new. Along the way I passed waterfalls, alpine meadows, rocky trails and panoramic viewpoints. It was both a physical challenge and a unique opportunity to experience the beauty and diversity of the Alps.
